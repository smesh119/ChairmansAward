Congratulations to our 2016 Chairman’s Award winners
Ten years ago, we initiated the Red Hat Chairman's Award, a company-wide recognition award that celebrates the exceptional work of associates around the world. Chairman’s Award winners are true leaders - they live our core values, create a positive customer experience, promote collaboration, and demonstrate expertise in their fields.

This year, we received close to an astounding 300 nominations for 185 Red Hat associates, which means many of them were nominated multiple times! These submissions were filled with stories about Red Hatters' tremendous work ethic, their passion for open source, and their dedication to the growth and success of our company. The Chairman’s Award is a peer-nominated program, and we want to thank those of you who took the time to recognize your colleagues for this prestigious honor.

Celebrating our winners

As part of the award process, each winner and a guest are invited to attend the Red Hat Summit, being held this June in San Francisco, California. We will host an awards banquet in their honor, where they will be joined by executive management and senior leaders to celebrate their achievements. We look forward to sharing the winners' stories at the Summit and in the coming months.

On behalf of the Corporate Leadership Team, I’d like to thank our winners for the great work they do for Red Hat every day. Please join me in congratulating our 2016 Chairman's Award winners!
