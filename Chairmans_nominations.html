Nominations are officially open for the 2017 Red Hat Chairman’s Award!

> Nominate a fellow Red Hatter by Tuesday, October 18

About the award

The Chairman’s Award recognizes a group of outstanding associates each year from around the world for their leadership and dedication to our company. It’s a peer-nominated program, which gives you the opportunity to highlight the talent and hard work of your coworkers.

This year’s award winners will be honored during Red Hat Summit in May, and recognized throughout the year both inside and outside the company. They’ll serve as global ambassadors of our culture, joining the group of previous award winners who have helped guide Red Hat to success.

How to nominate

To nominate a Red Hatter, review the nomination rules [1] and fill out the nomination form [2]. We want to hear your story – why should your nominee receive the Chairman's Award?

Every nominee requires two sponsors, so you’ll need to work with another associate to submit the form. Nominations close Tuesday, October 18, and winners will be notified in early January.

Learn more about the award and nominate. [3]
